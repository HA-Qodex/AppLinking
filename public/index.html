Android App Linking

This website is linked with an Android app using Android App Links.

What is Android App Linking?

Android App Linking allows your app to handle URLs that point to your website. When users click links associated with your domain, your app opens instead of the browser.

Setup Requirements

  • App must declare intent filters with autoVerify=true
  • Publish a Digital Asset Links file on your website
  • App must be signed with the release certificate
